History

And to put in historical context, it is interesting to mention the case of the first officially registered NTF, it is a meme known as Pepe the frog. Yes, that's right, a meme and that happened in 2016, they were initially inspired as digital art, and in this case the image was edited as a digital card assimilating the cards from the popular game YuGiOh. Although going back a bit, for the year 2012-2013 with the well-known Colored Coins, digital currencies that could be customized with different colors in addition to working with the Bitcoin blockchain, and they worked as cryptocurrencies other than bitcoin, the reason why They are not considered as NFT's because it did not fulfill the property of non-fungibility, that is, it did not have a unique and unrepeatable property, although historically they were pioneers because they shared some of the characteristics.

For the year 2017, in the Ethereum Blockckain the "Cryptokitties" were created, which are illustrations of cats with different customization characteristics and this is reflected in its code, curiously it had an objective as an entertainment game. NFT as art ... and other areas.

Although initially it was aimed at artists with their images, it is true that it transcends its use in other areas, such as: medicine, production lines (lots), education, real estate, events, metaverses. Etc.

In the case of the educational field, it can be used for certificates, diplomas and university degrees, as well as being absolute owners without depending on the university instance. 

For the field of medicine there is an initiative in Mexico called Genobank, which issues permission tokens for researchers to access our genetic information, its operation in general basically is when a person gives a genetic sample to a company for purposes such as Knowing their descent or ancestral origin, this company becomes the owner of the information, this last section is what defines the token, because this is how they manage to sell and obtain some type of profit. Cases like this it is clear the potential that this new technology has and the possibilities of use.
